Welcome

My research focuses on the development of targeted cancer therapies. To identify suitable enzymes for this purpose, I investigate bacterial defence systems, particularly CRISPR-Cas. To enable delivery, I currently develop electroporation-based techniques. In parallel, I am establishing advanced three-dimensional cancer models to improve experimental relevance and therapeutic screening.

This research is supported by NWO and KWF, with patient engagement via Longkanker Nederland.
