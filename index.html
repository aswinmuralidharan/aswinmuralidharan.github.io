Aswin Muralidharan

I am a postdoctoral scientist at Delft University of Technology, where I work on microscopic investigation of intracellular transport.

I did my PhD at Product and Process Engineering research group at Delft University of Technology, where I was advised by Pouyan Boukany and Michiel Kreutzer. The core research during my PhD focused on electrotransfer of exogeneous biomolecules to mammalian cells. During my Master's program, I worked on high frame rate particle image velocimetry at the group of Sa≈°a Kenjere≈°. I've assisted in graduate level courses Applied Numerical Methods and Applied Transport Phenomena.
