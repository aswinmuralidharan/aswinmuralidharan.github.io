Aswin Muralidharan

I am a postdoctoral scientist at Delft University of Technology with Stan Brouns, where I work on bacterial defense systems.

Previously, I studied biomolecule electrotransfer to mammalian cells and developed efficient electrotransfer devices with Pouyan Boukany and Michiel Kreutzer. The core research during my PhD focused on electrotransfer of exogenous biomolecules to mammalian cells. I also studied flow in cardiovascular mimicking systems using high frame rate particle image velocimetry, ultrasound particle image velocimetry and computational fluid dynamics with Sa≈°a Kenjere≈°.
